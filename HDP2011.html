Harambee Dinner - 2011

WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 28, 2011

Harambee is Swahili for “Let us all pull together” and originated in the country of Kenya as a slogan for national unity. Today we use this slogan as a way to unite our campus community as we celebrate HARAMBEE'S 25TH ANNIVERSARY of cultural enrichment and diversity that is represented among our students, faculty, and staff.
