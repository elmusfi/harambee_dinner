Harambee Dinner Programs Elizabeth Rose Wheeler Encoder/Editor

Produced as final project in DIGIT 110 class at Penn State Behrend.

MCC is the initialism for the Multi-Cultural Council, a student-led governing body of Penn State Behrend that is responsible for hosting the Harambee Dinners. SGA is the initialism for the Student Government Association, a student-led governing body of Penn State Behrend. NSBE is the acronym for National Society of Black Engineers, a club of Penn State Behrend and an organization under the MCC. ABC is the initialism for Association of Black Collegians, a club of Penn State Behrend and an organization under the MCC. HRPC is the initialism for Human Relations Programming Council, a club of Penn State Behrend and an organization under the MCC. ISO is the initialism for International Student Organization, a club of Penn State Behrend and an organization under the MCC. OACS is the initialism for Organization of Caribbean and African Students, a club of Penn State Behrend and an organization under the MCC. ASO is the initialism for Asian Student Organization, a club of Penn State Behrend and an organization under the MCC. MSA is the initialism for Muslim Student Organization, a club of Penn State Behrend and an organization under the MCC. OLAS is the acronym for Organization of Latin American Students, a club of Penn State Behrend and an organization under the MCC. Trigon is the former name of a club of Penn State Behrend and an organization under the MCC. Currently the club is called Gender and Sexuality Equality with the acronym of GASE. SASO is the acronym for South Asian Student Organization, a club of Penn State Behrend and an organization under the MCC. WT is the initialism for Women Today, a club of Penn State Behrend and an organization under the MCC. CCC is the initialism for Cultural Cooking Club, a club of Penn State Behrend and an organization under the MCC. AAUW is the initialism for American Association of University Women, a club of Penn State Behrend and an organization under the MCC. CCAC is the initialism for Chinese Cultural Advancement Club, a club of Penn State Behrend and an organization under the MCC.

These groups of text are dinner programs for a dinner event called Harambee. Hosted every fall semester at Penn State Behrend by the MCC. These programs traditionally are left on the dinner tables at the event for patrons to look through, but are ultimately to throw away. The event has been going on for 32 years in 2018, but only 8 programs have been salvaged.

Dinner Programs are held at Penn State Behrend Library Archives.

Menus and Thank You sections have been made into lists. All typos have been corrected. Only clubs have been given xmlids.
